A LITTLE BIT ABOUT US...

1What does KRILA mean?

Krila means wings in Serbo-Croatian. The Serbo-Croatian language is a South Slavic language, spoken mainly in Croatia, Serbia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Montenegro and parts of Kosovo.

2What is KRILA's intention?

Our purpose is to support companies in the establishment from health and not from disease. We are used to create processes of intervention to diseases and not of establishment, promotion and revention of health.
